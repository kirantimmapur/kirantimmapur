About Me

I am a third-year Electrical & Electronics Engineering student at Basaveshwar Engineering College, Bagalkot — building on a strong foundation earned through a Diploma in Electrical Engineering.

As Chair of BEC IEEE CASS (Circuits and Systems Society), I lead student initiatives, organize technical events, and bridge the gap between academic learning and industry application for my peers.

My focus lies in practical, impact-driven engineering — from assistive technology for the visually impaired to solar-powered EV charging systems. I aspire to contribute meaningfully to the electrical, industrial, and renewable energy sectors.
